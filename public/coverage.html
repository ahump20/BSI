Why BSI Exists

The Opportunity

Millions of fans follow college baseball, FCS football, and Group of Five programs. These sports generate real passion and real revenue—they deserve real analytics.

Complete box scores for every D1 baseball game. Deep analysis for FCS programs. Coverage that treats every team's fans like they matter—because they do.

This is the platform we always wanted. So we built it.

Our Approach

BSI covers every D1 baseball program. We track FCS and Group of Five football with the same rigor as the SEC. Every team gets real data, real analysis, and real coverage.

We publish complete box scores for college baseball. We cite our sources. We never fabricate data or present projections as facts.

The founder has personal loyalties (Longhorns, Cardinals, Grizzlies, Titans)—but professional coverage remains objective. Being a fan doesn't mean being a homer.
