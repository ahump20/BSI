Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

Effective Date: November 25, 2025

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how Blaze Sports Intel ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use blazesportsintel.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR (for EU visitors), CCPA (for California residents), and COPPA (for children under 13).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password
  • Game Data: Game saves, leaderboard submissions, player names (initials or usernames only)
  • User Preferences: Display settings, favorite teams, notification preferences
  • Communications: When you contact us, we collect the information you provide

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: We use Cloudflare Analytics (privacy-preserving) to collect:
    • Page views and session duration
    • Device type and browser information
    • Geographic location (country/region level only)
    • Referral sources
  • Performance Metrics: Web Vitals data for improving site performance
  • Error Logs: Technical error data via Sentry (IP addresses anonymized)

1.3 Third-Party Sports Data

We display publicly available sports statistics from:

  • SportsDataIO (professional sports statistics)
  • MLB StatsAPI (Major League Baseball data)
  • ESPN API (college and professional sports)
  • Perfect Game (youth baseball tournaments - publicly listed data only)

Note: We do not collect personal information about athletes. All displayed data is publicly available and sourced from official providers.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Age Restriction: Our mobile baseball game is designed for general audiences but does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

2.1 Information Collection from Children

  • We do NOT collect names, email addresses, or other personal information from users we know to be under 13
  • Game saves are stored locally on the device or anonymously in the cloud
  • Leaderboards use player-chosen usernames or initials only (no real names)
  • We do not use tracking cookies for children

2.2 Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:

  • Review any personal information collected from their child
  • Request deletion of their child's information
  • Refuse further collection of their child's information

To exercise these rights, submit a privacy request.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU Visitors)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

6.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know: Request disclosure of personal information collected, used, and shared
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

Privacy Contact:
Submit a Privacy Request

Response Time: We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA)

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Cloudflare Protection: DDoS protection and Web Application Firewall
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability scanning
  • Anonymization: IP addresses and sensitive data anonymized where possible

Note: No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
