2025 Conference Overview

The Pac-12 Conference remains one of college baseball's premier leagues, featuring elite programs like Oregon State (3 national titles), Stanford, UCLA, and Arizona. The conference annually sends multiple teams to the NCAA Tournament and produces significant MLB draft talent.

With conference realignment, the Pac-12 is entering a new era. The remaining members are committed to maintaining baseball excellence while the conference rebuilds. The 2025 season features Oregon State as the clear favorite, with Stanford and UCLA projected as the primary challengers.
