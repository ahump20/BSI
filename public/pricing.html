Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.
What happens after my free trial?
After your 14-day free trial, you'll be automatically charged for the Pro plan unless you cancel. You'll receive a reminder email 3 days before your trial ends.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe payment processing. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a full refund within the first 7 days of your paid subscription if you're not satisfied. Contact support@blazesportsintel.com for assistance.
