The sports analytics industry is growing at 20%+ annually, driven by professional teams' investments in Statcast, TrackMan, and biomechanics tracking. MLB captures over 1,200 data points per pitch—spin rate, exit velocity, launch angle, catch probability, and dozens more. This analytical depth transformed how professional baseball is played, coached, and consumed.

College baseball exists in an analytics vacuum. The 300+ D1 programs—with devoted fanbases that drove 1.5 million viewers to the 2024 College World Series—still rely primarily on manual charting and basic box scores. The analytical infrastructure that revolutionized MLB hasn't reached the college level, creating a gap between what fans want and what they can access.

BSI targets this gap: bringing professional-grade analytics to college baseball's passionate fanbase. Our goal is 10,000 paying subscribers within three years—approximately $1 million in annual recurring revenue serving an audience that's been underserved despite clear demand.

  • Pro ($29/mo): Advanced statistics, predictive models, recruiting intel

API licensing and custom solutions for athletic departments, media outlets, and sports networks.
