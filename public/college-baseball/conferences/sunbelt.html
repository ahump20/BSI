Conference History

Rising Sun in the South

The Sun Belt Conference has grown from a mid-major afterthought to a legitimate baseball power, headlined by Coastal Carolina's stunning 2016 national championship run. The Chanticleers' title proved that Sun Belt programs can compete with anyone when the lights are brightest.

With the additions of James Madison, Marshall, and Old Dominion in 2024, the conference has expanded its geographic footprint and competitive depth. Programs like Louisiana, Southern Miss, and South Alabama have built strong regional followings and consistently challenge for NCAA Tournament bids.

The Sun Belt's emphasis on developing talent has produced a steady stream of MLB draft picks, with players like Coastal Carolina's Andrew Benintendi (2015 Golden Spikes Award) proving that elite prospects can be found outside the Power Five conferences.
