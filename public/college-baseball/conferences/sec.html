Conference History

The Premier Baseball Conference

The Southeastern Conference has established itself as college baseball's most dominant league, producing more national champions, College World Series appearances, and MLB draft picks than any other conference. Since expanding to 16 teams with the additions of Texas and Oklahoma in 2024, the SEC's depth has only intensified.

With programs like LSU (6 national titles), Texas (6 national titles), Vanderbilt (2), Florida (1), Ole Miss (1), and South Carolina (2), the SEC boasts an unmatched pedigree. The conference regularly sends 8-10 teams to the NCAA Tournament, often claiming multiple regional hosts and national seeds.
