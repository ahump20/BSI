Conference History

Rising in the West

The Western Athletic Conference has evolved significantly in recent years, building a competitive baseball league with programs spread across the American West. From the desert heat of Phoenix to the Pacific Northwest, WAC baseball offers a unique blend of emerging programs and established contenders.

Grand Canyon has emerged as the flagship program, consistently competing for NCAA Tournament berths and producing MLB draft picks. Sacramento State brings decades of West Coast baseball tradition, while Texas schools like Tarleton State and Abilene Christian add depth and regional rivalries to the conference.
