Conference History

Coastal Excellence

The Atlantic Coast Conference has been a consistent force in college baseball, producing national champions and perennial contenders. Virginia's 2015 national title capped a remarkable run, while programs like Miami, Florida State, and North Carolina have deep traditions of CWS success.

With expansion bringing in Stanford, Cal, and SMU in 2024, the ACC has strengthened its position as one of baseball's elite conferences. Wake Forest has emerged as the conference's flagship program in recent years, reaching Omaha consistently and developing MLB-caliber talent.
