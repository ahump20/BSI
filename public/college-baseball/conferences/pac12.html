Conference History

West Coast Legacy

The Pac-12's baseball history is rich with tradition, led by Oregon State's three national championships (2006, 2007, 2018). USC's 12 titles make them college baseball's most decorated program historically, though they've now moved to the Big Ten.

The rebuilt Pac-12 maintains its commitment to West Coast baseball. Oregon State remains a national powerhouse under Pat Casey's legacy, while the new member schools bring strong regional baseball programs into the fold. The conference's commitment to player development continues with impressive MLB draft representation.
