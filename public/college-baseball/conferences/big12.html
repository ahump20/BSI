Conference History

A Conference on the Rise

The Big 12 has transformed its baseball profile through aggressive conference realignment and program investment. With the 2024 expansion adding Arizona, Arizona State, BYU, Cincinnati, Colorado, Houston, UCF, and Utah, the conference now spans 16 teams with diverse baseball traditions.

TCU's remarkable 2023 CWS runner-up finish—falling to LSU in the finals—announced the Big 12's arrival on college baseball's biggest stage. Programs like Texas Tech, Oklahoma State, and West Virginia continue to develop strong player pipelines, while newcomers Arizona State and Houston bring additional baseball pedigree.
