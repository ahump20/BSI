Conference History

A Conference Reborn

Conference USA was founded in 1995 and has seen constant membership flux throughout its history. The conference has produced notable baseball programs and MLB talent, though it has never claimed a national championship.

The 2023-24 realignment was the most dramatic in conference history. UAB, Rice, UTSA, North Texas, Charlotte, and FAU departed for the American Athletic Conference. Southern Miss, Marshall, and Old Dominion left for the Sun Belt. This exodus forced C-USA to rebuild almost entirely.

The new-look Conference USA features an interesting mix: established programs like LA Tech and FIU, rising FCS powers making the FBS jump (Jacksonville State, Kennesaw State, Sam Houston), and independents seeking conference homes (Liberty, New Mexico State).
