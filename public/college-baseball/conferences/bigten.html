Conference History

Midwest Meets West Coast

The Big Ten's baseball profile transformed dramatically with the 2024 additions of USC and UCLA. USC brings 12 national championships to the conference, instantly making the Big Ten one of college baseball's most historically decorated leagues.

Traditional Big Ten powers like Indiana, Michigan, and Maryland have consistently produced NCAA Tournament teams and MLB talent. The Hoosiers have been the conference's flagship program, making multiple CWS appearances and developing players like Kyle Schwarber and Alex Dickerson.
