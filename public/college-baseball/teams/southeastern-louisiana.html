Program History

Southeastern Louisiana University has one of the strongest baseball traditions in the Southland Conference. The Lions play their home games at Alumni Field in Hammond, Louisiana, located between New Orleans and Baton Rouge.

The program has won multiple conference championships and made numerous NCAA Tournament appearances. Southeastern Louisiana has produced over 60 MLB draft picks and maintains strong recruiting ties throughout the Gulf South region.
