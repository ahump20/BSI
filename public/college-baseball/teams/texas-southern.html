HBCU Heritage

Texas Southern University, founded in 1927 in Houston, Texas, is one of the largest HBCUs in the nation. As a proud Historically Black College or University, TSU has a distinguished legacy of academic achievement and athletic excellence. The Tigers compete in the Southwestern Athletic Conference (SWAC), one of the premier athletic conferences for HBCUs. Located in the heart of Houston's Third Ward, Texas Southern has produced numerous professional athletes and continues to serve as a beacon of opportunity for students from across the country. The baseball program carries forward this tradition, competing fiercely in conference play and developing student-athletes who excel both on the diamond and in their academic pursuits. Houston's rich baseball culture provides an excellent backdrop for Tiger baseball, drawing talent from one of the nation's most baseball-crazed metropolitan areas.
