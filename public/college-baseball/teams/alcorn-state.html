Program History

Alcorn State University, located in Lorman, Mississippi, is the oldest public historically black land-grant institution in the United States, founded in 1871. The Braves baseball program carries forward this legacy of excellence.

The program has won multiple SWAC championships and has produced professional players who have represented Alcorn at the highest levels of baseball. The rural Mississippi location provides a tight-knit community atmosphere focused on academic and athletic development.

Alcorn State continues to recruit talented student-athletes from Mississippi and surrounding states who value the combination of academic opportunity and competitive HBCU baseball.
