REST API Endpoints

Clean, documented endpoints. Rate-limited for reliability. Integrate with your existing tools or build custom applications.

GET

/api/v1/players/{id}

Complete player profiles with batting/pitching splits, career stats, and trend data.

GET

/api/v1/teams/{school}/roster

Full roster data including eligibility, position, and performance metrics.

GET

/api/v1/games/live

Real-time scoring updates for all games currently in progress.

GET

/api/v1/standings/{conference}

Conference standings with RPI, strength of schedule, and tiebreaker info.

GET

/api/v1/games/{id}/boxscore

Complete box scores with batting lines, pitching lines, and play-by-play.

GET

/api/v1/search?query={name}

Search players, teams, and games across all covered programs.
