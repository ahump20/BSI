Fair Market Value Methodology

Performance Component (50%)

Composite grade derived from production metrics, efficiency stats, and advanced analytics. Elite performers (90+) receive exponential value increases.

Exposure Component (25%)

Program tier, conference strength, media market size, and TV appearance frequency. Blue blood programs command 50%+ premium.

Social Component (15%)

Total followers across platforms weighted by engagement rate. Mega influencers (500K+) receive 80% multiplier boost.

Position Multiplier

QBs (2.5x), EDGE (1.75x), OT (1.6x), WR (1.45x), CB (1.4x). Reflects market scarcity and positional impact.
