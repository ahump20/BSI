California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Notice

Blaze Sports Intel Does Not Sell Personal Information

We want to be completely transparent: Blaze Sports Intel does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We never have, and we never will.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to opt out of the "sale" of their personal information. Because Blaze Sports Intel does not engage in the sale of personal information as defined by the CCPA, there is no need to opt out.

What We Do With Your Data

  • Essential Operations: We use your data only to provide and improve our sports analytics services
  • Analytics: We use privacy-preserving analytics (Cloudflare) that don't identify individual users
  • No Third-Party Marketing: We never share your information with advertisers or marketing companies
  • No Data Brokers: We never sell or provide your information to data brokers

Your CCPA Rights

Even though we don't sell your data, California residents still have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You can request what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • Right to Delete: You can request that we delete your personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We won't discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights
