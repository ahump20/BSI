Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

Effective Date: November 9, 2025

What Are Cookies? Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and improve functionality. This Cookie Policy explains how Blaze Sports Intel uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

1.1 Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. You cannot disable these cookies.

  • Session Management: Maintain your logged-in state
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Preferences: Remember your settings (theme, language)
  • Game Saves: Store your game progress locally

1.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our site. You can opt out of these cookies.

  • Cloudflare Analytics: Privacy-preserving analytics (no personal identifiers)
  • Web Vitals: Performance monitoring (page load speed, responsiveness)
  • Error Tracking: Sentry error logs (IP addresses anonymized)

1.3 Cookies We Do NOT Use

  • Advertising Cookies: We do not serve ads or use advertising cookies
  • Third-Party Tracking: We do not allow third-party tracking cookies
  • Social Media Pixels: We do not use Facebook, Twitter, or other social pixels

3. Other Tracking Technologies

3.1 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

  • Cache sports statistics for offline viewing
  • Store game saves (as backup to cookies)
  • Remember user preferences

3.2 Session Storage

  • Temporary data storage for active browsing sessions
  • Cleared when you close your browser tab

3.3 Web Beacons

We do NOT use web beacons, tracking pixels, or similar invisible tracking technologies.

4. Third-Party Services and Cookies

4.1 Cloudflare

  • Purpose: Hosting, CDN, security, analytics
  • Privacy: Cloudflare Analytics is privacy-preserving (no personal identifiers)
  • Policy: Cloudflare Cookie Policy

4.2 Sentry

  • Purpose: Error monitoring and performance tracking
  • Privacy: IP addresses are anonymized before storage
  • Policy: Sentry Privacy Policy

4.3 Sports Data Providers

  • SportsDataIO, MLB StatsAPI, ESPN API, Perfect Game: We receive data from these providers but do not share your cookies or personal information with them

5. Your Cookie Choices and Controls

Manage Your Cookie Preferences

You can control which cookies we use (except essential cookies, which are required for the site to function).

5.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies (essential + analytics)
  • Reject optional cookies (essential only)
  • Customize your preferences

5.2 Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

5.3 Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect the "Do Not Track" browser signal. When DNT is enabled:

  • We disable all optional analytics cookies
  • Essential cookies remain active (required for functionality)

5.4 Mobile Devices

On mobile browsers:

  • iOS Safari: Settings → Safari → Block All Cookies
  • Android Chrome: Settings → Site settings → Cookies

6. Impact of Blocking Cookies

6.1 If You Block Essential Cookies

The site will not function properly. You may experience:

  • Inability to log in or maintain sessions
  • Loss of preferences on each visit
  • Game saves not persisting
  • Security features not working

6.2 If You Block Analytics Cookies

The site will function normally, but:

  • We cannot measure site performance
  • We cannot identify and fix issues
  • We cannot optimize user experience

7. Children's Privacy and Cookies

We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 via cookies
  • Essential cookies used for children are limited to game saves (stored locally)
  • Analytics cookies are disabled for users identified as under 13
  • Parents can request deletion of any cookie data by contacting: ahump20@outlook.com

8. International Visitors (GDPR Compliance)

For visitors from the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements:

8.1 Consent

  • We obtain explicit consent before setting non-essential cookies
  • Consent is freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous
  • You can withdraw consent at any time via our cookie banner or settings

8.2 Lawful Basis

  • Essential Cookies: Legitimate interest (necessary for service provision)
  • Analytics Cookies: Consent (you must opt in)

8.3 Your GDPR Rights

  • Right to access cookie data
  • Right to erasure (delete all cookies)
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact: ahump20@outlook.com

9. Cookie Lifespan and Deletion

9.1 Session Cookies

  • Deleted automatically when you close your browser
  • Used for temporary session management

9.2 Persistent Cookies

  • Remain until expiration date or manual deletion
  • Used for preferences and long-term game saves
  • Maximum lifespan: 1 year (then renewed if you consent)

9.3 Manual Deletion

You can delete cookies at any time through:

  • Browser settings (Clear browsing data)
  • Our cookie management tool (click "Manage Cookie Settings" above)
