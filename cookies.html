1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide better service, remember preferences, and analyze usage patterns to improve our sports analytics platform.

Quick Cookie Facts

  • Cookies cannot access personal files on your device
  • They cannot install malware or viruses
  • You have full control over cookie settings
  • Essential cookies are required for basic site functionality

3. DETAILED COOKIE DESCRIPTIONS

3.1 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.

  • session_id: Maintains your session while browsing
  • security_token: CSRF protection for secure form submissions
  • user_preferences: Language, timezone, and accessibility settings
  • cookie_consent: Remembers your cookie preferences

3.2 Analytics Cookies (Consent Required)

Help us understand how visitors use our site to improve services.

  • _ga (Google Analytics): Distinguishes unique users
  • _gid (Google Analytics): Distinguishes users in 24-hour period
  • cf_analytics (Cloudflare): Performance and security metrics
  • blaze_analytics: Custom sports data usage analytics

3.3 Functionality Cookies (Consent Required)

Enable enhanced functionality and personalization features.

  • team_preferences: Your favorite teams (Cardinals, Titans, etc.)
  • display_settings: Dashboard layout and theme preferences
  • notification_prefs: Alert and notification settings
  • api_settings: API usage preferences and rate limit tracking

3.4 Third-Party Cookies

Set by external services we use:

  • Sports Data Providers: Authentication tokens for MLB, NCAA APIs
  • Cloudflare: Security and performance optimization
  • Google Services: Analytics and reCAPTCHA (when used)

6. IMPACT OF DISABLING COOKIES

What Happens When You Disable Cookies?

  • Essential Cookies: Website may not function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: We can't improve services based on usage data
  • Functionality Cookies: Limited personalization and saved preferences

7. DO NOT TRACK

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals. When DNT is enabled:

  • No analytics cookies are set
  • No tracking pixels are loaded
  • Essential cookies only for functionality
  • Third-party tracking disabled
