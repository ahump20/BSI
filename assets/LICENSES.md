## Replacement Checklist

Before committing new media:

1. **Provenance** – Record prompt/tool/artist and attach license summary below.
2. **Originality** – Confirm no references to Backyard Baseball, MLB clubs, or real athletes.
3. **Format** – Prefer SVG or compressed WebP/PNG ≤ 100KB.
4. **Accessibility** – Provide alt text or captions when surfaced in UI.
5. **Review** – Legal + product sign-off prior to merge.
