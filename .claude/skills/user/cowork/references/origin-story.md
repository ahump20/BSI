## BSI — The Mission

BSI covers what mainstream sports media overlooks: the athletes, programs, and markets outside the East and West Coast spotlight. The gap between interest in college and amateur sports and access to meaningful analytics is the product — old-school scouting instinct fused with new-school sabermetrics, powered by AI tooling that makes the depth accessible.

**Vision over grievance governs all public-facing BSI content.** Show the work. The absence elsewhere becomes obvious through contrast. Never name competitors directly — let the coverage gap speak for itself.
