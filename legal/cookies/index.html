Important Notice: This Cookie Policy explains how Blaze Sports Intel uses cookies and similar technologies. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy and our Privacy Policy. You can manage cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, track usage patterns, and provide personalized experiences.

1.1 Types of Technologies We Use

  • Cookies: Text files stored in your browser
  • LocalStorage: Browser-based storage for preferences and session data
  • SessionStorage: Temporary storage cleared when browser closes
  • Web Beacons: Invisible images tracking page views (used in analytics)
  • IndexedDB: Client-side database for offline capabilities

3.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional - Requires Consent)

Purpose: Help us understand how visitors use the site to improve functionality.

  • Cloudflare Analytics: Anonymized page views, user flows, performance metrics

  • Performance Monitoring: Measure page load times and API response times

Data Collected: Page views, referring sites, device type (desktop/mobile), browser type, general geographic region (city-level, not precise location)

3.3 Preference Cookies (Optional - Requires Consent)

Purpose: Remember your settings and personalize your experience.

  • Favorite Teams: Cardinals (MLB), Titans (NFL), Longhorns (NCAA), Grizzlies (NBA)
  • Sports Order: Baseball → Football → Basketball → Track & Field
  • Display Settings: Dark mode, chart preferences, dashboard layout

5. AI & ML COOKIE USAGE

5.1 Workers AI Analytics

When you use our AI Copilot feature (/copilot), we store conversation history in localStorage for 90 days to improve AI responses. This includes:

  • Semantic Search Queries: Processed using Cloudflare Workers AI (@cf/baai/bge-base-en-v1.5)
  • AI Chat Sessions: Questions and AI-generated responses using @cf/meta/llama-3.1-8b-instruct
  • 3D Visualization Interactions: Babylon.js field interactions and camera positions
  • User Feedback: Thumbs up/down on AI responses (anonymized)

AI Data Usage: Your AI Copilot queries are processed in real-time and not used to train external AI models. Conversation history is stored locally in your browser and deleted after 90 days or when you clear browser data.

6. HOW TO MANAGE COOKIES

6.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit blazesportsintel.com, you'll see our cookie consent banner (bottom-left, glassmorphism design). You can:

  • Accept All: Enable all cookies for full functionality
  • Decline Optional: Only essential cookies (some features may be limited)
  • Customize: Choose specific cookie categories (coming soon)
  • Change Preferences: Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer anytime

6.2 Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies

Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent the website from functioning properly. Features like live score updates, API access, and personalized dashboards require cookies to operate.

6.3 Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect browser Do Not Track signals. When DNT is enabled, we:

  • Disable all analytics cookies
  • Anonymize IP addresses in server logs
  • Do not track cross-site behavior
  • Still collect essential data for service functionality

9. GDPR & CCPA RIGHTS

9.1 EU Users (GDPR)

  • Right to Object: Opt-out of analytics and preference cookies
  • Right to Access: Request copy of data collected via cookies
  • Right to Erasure: Delete all non-essential cookies and data
  • Right to Data Portability: Export preferences in JSON format

9.2 California Users (CCPA)

  • Right to Know: What cookies are used and why
  • Right to Delete: Remove all non-essential cookies
  • Right to Opt-Out: Decline sale of personal information (we don't sell)
  • Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of cookie choices

Exercise Your Rights: Email privacy@blazesportsintel.com with "Cookie Data Request" in the subject line.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA)

We do not knowingly collect cookies or personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided information, contact us immediately at privacy@blazesportsintel.com.
