Our Commitment to AI Transparency: Blaze Sports Intel believes in complete transparency regarding our use of artificial intelligence and machine learning. This page discloses all AI models, their purposes, data usage, and limitations. We prioritize ethical AI practices and user control.

1. INTRODUCTION TO OUR AI USAGE

Blaze Sports Intel uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance sports analytics, provide intelligent search capabilities, and deliver predictive insights. All AI usage is designed to complement, not replace, human sports analysis and expertise.

1.1 Core AI Principles

  • Transparency: Clear disclosure of all AI models and their purposes
  • User Control: Ability to opt-out of AI features while maintaining core functionality
  • No Hidden Training: Your data is not used to train external AI models without consent
  • Accuracy Disclaimer: AI predictions are estimates, not guarantees
  • Human Oversight: Critical decisions reviewed by sports analysts

3. HOW WE USE AI RESPONSIBLY

3.1 Transparency Commitments

  • AI Labeling: All AI-generated content clearly marked with "AI-Generated" badge
  • Human Review: Critical predictions reviewed by sports analysts before publication
  • Accuracy Metrics: Publish prediction accuracy rates (updated weekly)
  • Model Versioning: Track which AI model version generated each prediction
  • Explainability: Provide "Why this prediction?" explanations for AI insights

3.2 Ethical AI Practices

  • No Bias Amplification: Regular audits for racial, gender, or geographic bias in predictions
  • Fair Representation: Ensure balanced coverage across all teams and leagues
  • Responsible Prediction: No AI-driven gambling recommendations (prohibited in Texas)
  • Youth Protection: AI models do not profile minors in youth sports coverage
  • Data Minimization: Collect only necessary data for AI functionality

3.3 User Control

You Control AI Usage:

  • Opt-Out Option: Disable AI Copilot in account settings
  • Data Deletion: Delete AI conversation history anytime
  • Feedback: Rate AI responses (thumbs up/down) to improve accuracy
  • Manual Mode: View raw sports data without AI interpretation
  • Export Data: Download your AI interaction history (JSON format)

4. AI LIMITATIONS & DISCLAIMERS

Important AI Disclaimers:

  • Not Professional Advice: AI predictions are for informational/entertainment purposes only
  • Accuracy Not Guaranteed: Sports outcomes are inherently unpredictable
  • Possible Errors: AI may generate incorrect statistics or hallucinate facts
  • Verify Critical Info: Always cross-check important data with official sources
  • No Financial Decisions: Do not use AI predictions for betting or financial decisions

4.1 Known AI Limitations

  • Recency Bias: AI may overweight recent performance in predictions
  • Injury Updates: AI predictions may lag real-time injury news (15-30 min delay)
  • Contextual Gaps: AI cannot account for locker room dynamics, coaching changes, weather impacts
  • Historical Data Dependency: Predictions less accurate for new players/teams with limited history
  • Language Model Hallucinations: Occasional generation of plausible-sounding but incorrect facts

5. AI TRAINING & YOUR DATA

5.1 What Data Trains Our AI?

  • Public Sports Data: MLB, NFL, NCAA, NBA statistics (publicly available)
  • Historical Games: 2020-2025 season data for trend analysis
  • Aggregated Usage Patterns: Anonymized search trends (no individual tracking)
  • Feedback Signals: User thumbs-up/down on AI responses (anonymized)

5.2 What Data Does NOT Train AI?

Privacy Protection Guarantee:

  • Your Personal Information: Email, name, phone never used for AI training
  • Individual Queries: Your specific AI Copilot questions not used to train models
  • Account Data: Login credentials, payment info, preferences never shared with AI providers
  • Location Data: Precise location never used for AI training
  • Youth Sports Data: Minor's information strictly excluded from all AI processing
