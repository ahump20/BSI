2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Minimal Data Collection Statement

We collect minimal personal information. Our platform is designed to display sports data without requiring user accounts, registration, or payment information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: Pages viewed, time spent, navigation patterns (via Cloudflare Analytics Engine - anonymized)
  • Device Information: IP address (anonymized), browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on anonymized IP address (country/state level only)
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for functionality, analytics cookies with your consent (see Cookie Policy)

2.3 Contact Form Submissions (Optional)

If you contact us via email or contact form:

  • Email address - For responding to your inquiry
  • Name - Optional, if you provide it
  • Message content - Your questions or feedback

Retention: Contact form data is deleted after your inquiry is resolved (typically 30-60 days).

2.4 AI Copilot Interactions

When you use our AI Sports Intelligence Copilot at /copilot:

  • Search Queries: Questions and prompts submitted to the AI system
  • Semantic Search: Query embeddings generated using @cf/baai/bge-base-en-v1.5
  • AI Responses: Generated insights using @cf/meta/llama-3.1-8b-instruct
  • Conversation History: Chat sessions stored for 90 days for quality improvement

Processing: All AI processing occurs on Cloudflare Workers AI infrastructure. Your data is NOT shared with third-party AI providers. See AI Disclosure for details.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • User account credentials (no account system exists)
  • Payment information (platform is free)
  • Social security numbers or government IDs
  • Health or medical information
  • Biometric data
  • Precise geolocation (GPS coordinates)
