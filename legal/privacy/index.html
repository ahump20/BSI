2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Data: Email address, username, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Sports preferences, notification settings
  • Communications: Support tickets, feedback, survey responses
  • API Usage: Requests made to our sports data APIs

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies (see Cookie Policy)

2.3 Sports Data Services

  • API Interactions: Queries made to sports data providers
  • Saved Preferences: Leagues monitored, custom alerts
  • Historical Searches: Past queries for personalization (retained 90 days)

2.4 AI Copilot Feature

  • Copilot Queries: Questions and prompts submitted to our AI Copilot at /copilot
  • Semantic Search: Search queries processed through Workers AI embeddings (@cf/baai/bge-base-en-v1.5)
  • RAG Insights: AI-generated responses using Cloudflare Workers AI (@cf/meta/llama-3.1-8b-instruct)
  • Conversation History: Copilot chat sessions (retained 90 days for quality improvement)
  • 3D Visualizations: Interactions with Babylon.js baseball/football/basketball field visualizations

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment information (processed by third-party providers)
  • Biometric data
  • Government identification numbers
  • Health or medical information

3. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process your information under the following legal bases:

3.1 GDPR (European Users)

3.2 CCPA (California Users)

7.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Access: Request copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: "Right to be forgotten"
  • Portability: Receive data in portable format
  • Restriction: Limit processing
  • Objection: Opt-out of certain processing

7.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)
