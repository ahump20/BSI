Why This Exists

Memphis to Texas. Cardinals, Titans, Longhorns, Grizzlies. Enough years watching to recognize the pattern: analytics platforms measure what's easy, not what wins.

NFL sidelines: coaches consulting models that ignore momentum. College programs: tools optimized for statistical elegance, not competitive reality. Front offices: spreadsheets measuring launch angle while missing situational awareness.

Three sports in high school. Years competing through Texas. But credibility doesn't come from playing—it comes from watching long enough to know what the tools miss.

Exit velocity is easy. Recognizing when a hitter adjusts under pressure isn't. Yards per play is convenient. Reading defensive response after turnovers requires context. Blaze measures what matters.
